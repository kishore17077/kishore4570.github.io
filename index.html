SSN COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

Sri Sivasubramaniya Nadar College of Engineering (SSNCE) is the outcome of the vision and initiative of Dr. Shiv Nadar, Chairman, HCL Technologies and a pioneer in the field of Information Technology. The Institution stands out as a premier center of higher learning with a mission of pursuing excellence in education and research. SSN institutions, with its diverse and dynamic community of students offer a distinctive combination of some of the finest undergraduate, postgraduate and research programmes. The campus has been interconnected through wired as well as wireless (Wi-Fi) Network. In addition, online access is provided to reference books and journals. This has enabled SSN Institutions to adopt innovative teaching & learning methodologies. The institution is certified with ISO and accredited by NBA and NAAC. SSN has been consistently proving its class by retaining the top position among Anna University affiliated Engineering Colleges in Tamilnadu.

SSN TROPHY

SSN trophy is the national level inter collegiate sports festival proudly organised by SSN College of Engineering for the past 17 years. An annual 4 day event, SSN trophy was first held in the year2003 with participants competing in Basketball, Badminton and Table Tennis. Since then the SSN Trophy has grown manifold and has become one of the most desired and appreciated sporting events across the country.

  • SSN trophy has a footfall over 1000 participants
  • 50+ Institutions from over 5 states across India
  • 13 events conducted across 8 sports
  • Cash Prizes and Awards worth Rs. 5 Lakhs

Over the years the Opening and closing ceremonies have been graced by various Indian Sportspersons such as Mr.Sharath Kamal, Mr.John Wright, Ms.Anita Paul, Shri.Robin Singh.We would proudly say that SSN Trophy has been a stepping stone for many currently successful sportspersons from other colleges have participated in SSN Trophy on their way to professional glory. This edition of the SSN Trophy is the edition and it is more special because it is the first year of the decade, we hope that it reaches a whole new level of grandeur, competition and excellence in sports.
